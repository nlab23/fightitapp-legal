# Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** 12/27/2025
**Owner:** Nibs Studio LLC
**Contact:** nibsstudio@proton.me

## Data We Collect
- **App data:** Craving logs, settings, and game progress are stored locally on your device. We do not run our own servers for this data.
- **Purchases:** In-app purchases are processed by Apple/Google. We do not receive your full payment details.
- **Notifications:** If you enable notifications, your preference is stored locally to deliver reminders.
- **No accounts:** We do not require account creation or login.

## How We Use Data
- To provide app features (track cravings, show progress, manage settings).
- To deliver optional notifications you enable.
- To process purchases via Apple/Google storefronts.

## Sharing
- We do **not** sell or share your personal data.
- Apple/Google receive only what is needed to process purchases.

## Data Storage & Retention
- Data remains on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes locally stored data.

## Permissions
- **Notifications** (optional): used to send reminders.  
No other permissions are required for core features.

## Your Choices
- Disable notifications in the app or device settings.
- Uninstall the app to remove local data.

## Children
This app is intended for adults seeking to quit nicotine and is not directed to users under 18. It is not designed for children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and have concerns, contact us at nibsstudio@proton.me

## Changes
- We may update this policy and will revise the “Effective Date” above when we do.
